About Event

Anatolian Blockchain Crypto Connect II

Anatolian Blockchain Crypto Connect II is a special side event of Cosmoverse 2023. This event provides an opportunity for project owners, entrepreneurs, investors, and valuable individuals from the crypto ecosystem to come together and network. The event aims to offer an enjoyable experience with a dynamic program taking place from 7 pm to 11 pm. Don't miss this opportunity to showcase your projects, meet investors, and expand your network. We bring the energy and excitement of Cosmoverse to our side event! Don't miss out on this opportunity to directly interact with these experienced individuals!
